These charts show various team data across the league and compare teams to eachother based on different metrics in order to give a sense of the relative performance of each team.

Standings

Points for, points against, and record for each team for the season. Teams are ordered left-to-right by their league rank. The bars are broken down by week so they show each team's points for and against for each week of the season as well as the total points for and against for the season. 

Weekly Projections

Scoring projections for each week of the season for each team in the league. This chart shows how the teams are predicted to stack up throughout the season.

Team Points

Projected points for/against and actual points for/against for each team for each week of the season. This gives a sense of how well a team performs compared to predictions.

Win Probability

Discrete probaility distribution for number of wins at the end of the season. The arrow denotes the maximum probability. The data is based on Yahoo! mathcup probabilities for each matchup of the season. This chart gives a good idea of which teams are favored to win more games by the end of the season.

Roster Performance

Total points scored for each team for each week of the season broken down by roster position. These charts show the strong and weak positions of each team's roster and how those positions have performed throughout the season. 

Bull Score

Bull score for each team for each week of the season and total bull score for each team for the season. Bull score is a measure of the amount of 'luck' a team has had throughout the season; for example, if the median score in the league for a week is 150 and a team scores 160 but loses, their bull score for the week is -10. Bull score is a way to keep track of which teams have been blessed or smited by the fantasy football gods.
