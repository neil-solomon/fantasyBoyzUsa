Legacy

Historical data for all of the seasons of Fantasy Boyz. Former teams with at least four seasons and all current teams are included in the legacy.

Regular Season Wins

Career regular season wins for each legacy team in Fantasy Boyz. The number of wins for each season is displayed inside the bar and the total career wins is displayed on top of the bar.

Regular Season Points

Career regular season points for each legacy team in Fantasy Boyz. The number of points for each season is displayed inside the bar and the total career points is displayed on top of the bar.

Post Season Wins

Career post season wins for each legacy team in Fantasy Boyz. The number of wins for each season is displayed inside the bar and the total career wins is displayed on top of the bar.

Championships

Career championships for each legacy team in Fantasy Boyz.  Perhaps the only stat that really matters. 

Final Standings

The final standing for each legacy team in Fantasy Boyz. The final standing for each season is displayed inside the bar and the career average final standing is displayed on top of the bar.

Week Top Score

A team achieves a top score when they score the most points in the league in a given week. This data includes both regular and post season games. The number of top scores for each season is displayed inside the bar and the total career top scores is displayed on top of the bar.

Big Games

A team achieves a big game when they score 25% more than the regular season average of the league champion that year. For example: in 2013 LA, the league champion, averaged 161.15 points during the regular season; so any score above 201.43 in 2013 is a big game. For the current season a big game is 25% more points than the league average for the season. This data includes both regular and post season games. The number of big games for each season is displayed inside the bar and the total career big games is displayed on top of the bar.
